📖 RULEBOOK
OVERVIEW

LumeKin is an asynchronous digital board game for 2–6 players. Travel a 60-square board, collecting and developing creatures called Kins, then battle in arenas at the end of each lap. 9 laps total — highest score wins.

THE BOARD

60 squares in a loop. Roll 1 or 2 dice per turn (your choice). On the Arena Side (sq. 46–60): 1 die only. Every lap ends at the Battleground (sq. 60) where all players fight in the arena.

KINS & STATS

18 Kins across 6 elements. Max 5 active Kins (unlimited storage with Stronghold, still max 5 in arena).

DMG — Raw damage
SPD — Attack order
PRE — Crit/miss chance
HP — Total health
GRD — Reduces damage
EVA — Dodge chance
GRT — Better at low HP
SYN — Bonus to next Kin
ELE — Element effect power

3 XP = level up (+1 DMG, GRD, SPD). Evolution Stone (12 Gold, Market) = instant evolution for Lv2+ Kins: +5 to all attack stats, +15 HP.

ELEMENTS & TYPE MATCHUPS
🔥 Fire — Burn
Extra damage each round. Strong vs Earth & Air.
💧 Water — Ricochet
Damage bounces to next Kin. Strong vs Fire & Dark.
⚡ Electric — Stun
Chance to skip opponent's attack. Strong vs Water & Air.
🌱 Earth — Life Steal
Regain % of damage as HP. Strong vs Water & Electric.
🌬️ Air — Execute
+50% damage when opponent <20% HP. Strong vs Earth & Dark.
🌑 Dark — Curse
Stacks -3% DEF/round · Explodes on death. Strong vs Fire & Electric.

Type multipliers: Strong = 1.5× · Neutral = 1.0× · Weak = 0.5×

LEADER SYSTEM

Your Leader always fights first in the arena. Each element gives a Board Ability (once per lap) and an Encounter Ability (when you land on an opponent).

🔥 Blaze — Roll never below 3
💧 Tide — Move forward or back
⚡ Surge — Roll 2 dice, pick 1
🌱 Roothold — Trigger square again
🌬️ Gale — Roll 3, take best 2
🌑 Veil — Reject next card drawn
ARENA FORMAT (9 LAPS)
Lap 1 — 1v1 Lap 2 — 2v2 Lap 3 — 3v3 Lap 4 — 4v4 Lap 5 — 5v5 ★ Lap 6 — 4v4 Lap 7 — 3v3 Lap 8 — 2v2 Lap 9 — 1v1
SCORING
Arena win Lap 1–2: 5p + 8 Gold
Arena win Lap 3–4: 10p + 5 Gold
Arena win Lap 5: 25p + 3 Gold
Arena win Lap 6–7: 15p + 2 Gold
Arena win Lap 8–9: 20p + 1 Gold
Glory Point sq.: 5p
Battle Kroneer win: 20p + 20 Gold
Stronghold upgrade: 8p
Evolved Kin: 5p each
Kin levels up: 3p each
BASE CAMP & STRONGHOLD

6 Base Camps chosen before the game. Landing on your own: all Kins +2 DMG/GRD, +5 HP, +3 Gold. Upgrade to Stronghold for 12 Gold (+8p). Stronghold doubles bonuses, allows unlimited Kin storage, and charges 10 Gold rent to opponents.
