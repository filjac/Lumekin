🏁 OVERVIEW

LumeKin is an asynchronous digital board game for 2–6 players. Players travel a 60-square board, collecting and developing creatures called Kins, then battle in an arena at the end of each lap. The game runs 9 laps with escalating arena formats, followed by a playoff. Highest score wins — but scoring leads only give an advantage, not victory.

HOW TO WIN — Collect Kins, develop them, win arena battles. After 9 laps a playoff begins. The playoff is single elimination — anyone can win regardless of score.
TURN STRUCTURE

On your turn, roll 1 or 2 dice (your choice) and move forward. On the Arena Side (squares 46–60), you may only roll 1 die. Land on a square and trigger its effect. If you reach square 60 (Battleground), an arena battle is triggered immediately and all other players are moved there too.

🗺️ THE BOARD

The board has 60 squares arranged in a loop with 4 sides. Three Dream Kin corners mark the transitions between sides. The final side is the Arena approach (1 die only).

LAYOUT — Side 1 (1–14) → Dream Kin corner (15) → Side 2 (16–29) → Dream Kin corner (30) → Side 3 (31–44) → Dream Kin corner (45) → Arena Side (46–59) → Battleground (60)

👑 LEADER SYSTEM & ABILITIES

APEX EVOLUTION

A Kin at Level 4 or higher can be evolved using an Evolution Stone (Market card, 12 Gold). Evolution is permanent and grants:

+15% of base stats (DMG, GRD, SPD, HP, PRE) · Unique Specialattack — 30% chance (modified by PRE) · 2.2× damage multiplier · Replaces critical hits entirely

RULES — Leader always fights. Fighter order = order Kins were collected (changeable via Mobilize or cards). If not enough Kins for format, random Kins from pool fill in permanently. All Kins fully heal after each lap.
DAMAGE FORMULA
Damage = (DMG − GRD × 0.6) × Type multiplier × RNG modifier
Miss (5–10%) = 0× · Normal (80%) = 1.0× · Critical (10–35%) = 1.8× · Apex Special (30%+) = 2.2×
PRE increases crit/special chance. GRT boosts performance at low HP. Apex Kins replace crits with their Specialattack.

🥇 PLAYOFF

After lap 9, the playoff begins. This is single elimination — anyone can win regardless of their score during the 9 laps. Kins heal fully between each match.

#1 SEED ADVANTAGES — The top seed gets to: (1) choose how many Kins to use (1–5) for the entire playoff, (2) choose which QF winner they face in the semi-final. These are the only advantages — stronger Kins from playing well are the real reward.
FEWER THAN 6 PLAYERS — Bots fill in to reach minimum 4 players. Bot difficulty scales with the lap number.
